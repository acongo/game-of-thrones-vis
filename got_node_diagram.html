Game of Thrones Kill Graph

Explore the links between killer and killed in this visualization of Game of Thrones' major characters. An arrow originating from character A and pointing to character B indicates character A killed character B. An isolated character with an arrow pointing to themselves indicates death by suicide, accident, or natural causes. The donut graph around each character indicates their favorite weapons of choice as a proportion of their total weapons used for murder (inclusive of kills against minor characters not shown in this diagram).
